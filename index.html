About Me

I'm Lily Burgham, an enthusiastic programmer, designer, gamer, musician and video editor based within Coventry, UK. I graduated with a first-class honors in Computer Games Programming (BSc) at De Montfort University in 2020. I am currently looking for work as a graduate/junior developer to progress into the games industry.

I have a passion for making art. Whether that's through designing a game and world for players to experience, composing music for people to listen to or editing videos to entertain others. I have a wide breadth of skills, specializing in programming, audio, game design and communicating with others. I have lots of experience coding and using game engines to create and design my own games, to playing games professionally as a leader of a professional esports team. I love blending my favourite interests- games and audio together to create audio engines or create rhythm/music games, or any work with audio within games.
