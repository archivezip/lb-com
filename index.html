About Me

I'm Lily Burgham, an enthusiastic programmer, designer, gamer, musician and video editor based within Coventry, UK.
I graduated with a first-class honors in Computer Games Programming (BSc) at De Montfort University in 2020.
I have a passion for making art. Whether that's through designing a world for players to experience, composing music or editing.
I have a wide breadth of skills, specializing in programming, audio, game design and communicating with others.
I have lots of experience coding and using game engines to create and design my own games, to playing games professionally as a leader of a professional esports team.
I love blending my favourite interests- games and audio together to create audio engines or create rhythm/music games, or any work with audio within games.
